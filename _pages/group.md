## Want to work with me?

I advise students enrolled in the Ph.D. and M.S. in Applied Statistics programs in Statistics at UW–Madison.
To apply to work with me as a Ph.D. student, please submit your application to [Wisconsin Statistics](https://stat.wisc.edu/graduate-admissions/phd-program/); I do not directly admit students to my group.
If you are interested in working with me, please mention that in your application.
That said, I am not involved in the admissions process nor can I comment or provide feedback on your application.

If you are enrolled in another Ph.D. program at Wisconsin and are interested in working with me, please consider adding an [M.S. in Applied Statistics](https://stat.wisc.edu/graduate-admissions/applied-statistics-option/).
I am happy to discuss potential courses and thesis ideas with you (and potentially serving as your MSAS advisor!).

I unfortunately do not offer internships, visiting scholar positions, or summer research postitions for students not enrolled at Wisconsin, regardless of fudning.


As the majority of my reserach involves Bayesian Statistics, I **strongly** advise students to take STAT 775 before joining my group.
